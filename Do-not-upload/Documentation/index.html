Capitol - Creative Multi-Purpose PSD Template

Capitol is a creative Multi-Purpose PSD Template designed by minimal style which focus on 2 basic colors black and white. It makes Capitol becomes exquisite and attracts the readers. With 2 types of homepage - one page & multi pages - and 17 variations of hero images, Capitol can be suitable for all type of business.

How To Edit PSD Files

In order to edit the file, you need to have Adobe Photoshop installed on your system, and the fonts mentioned in the credits installed.

As you can see on the right, the layers are well organized and gropued into folders, which make editing very easy.

So, all you have to do, is select the layer from the right, and then edit it with Adobe Photoshop.

That's all! Enjoy.
